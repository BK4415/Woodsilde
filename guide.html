Guide

How To Play

Slide the tiles into their goal positions by sliding them into the empty slot.

Controls

  • Tap a movable tile to slide it into the empty slot.
  • Drag from the outermost movable tile to push a whole row or column.
  • Pause any time using the top-right button.
  • Swipe from the left edge for Information, right edge for Dashboard.

Modes

  • Classic — numbers 1 to N in reading order.
  • Snake — numbers zig-zag across rows.
  • Spiral — numbers spiral inward from a corner.
  • Upside Down — numbers descend toward the top-left empty slot.

Photo Mode

Pick one of the preset photos or upload your own. The image is automatically center-cropped to fit the board. You can toggle tile numbers on or off during the game.

Performance Rating

Earn 1–3 stars based on your time and moves. Faster solves with fewer moves earn higher ratings.
